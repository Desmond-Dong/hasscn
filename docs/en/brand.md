:::info What is "Direct Support"?

`Direct support` refers to brands or protocol devices that users can easily and directly purchase in the China region.
Some open protocols (such as Zigbee, Z-Wave, Wi-Fi, etc.) cover many brands and cannot be listed one by one.
Only open standards that can be easily integrated are recorded. Products that require complex reverse engineering and simulated login are not included.

:::
