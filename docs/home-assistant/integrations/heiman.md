# Heiman

[Heiman](https://www.heimantech.com/) is a member of the Works with Home Assistant partner program for their Matter products. Heiman is committed to making sure their products are up-to-date and ready to use in Home Assistant.

Heiman Matter devices work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Matter integration in Home Assistant. As all connectivity happens locally, status updates and device control happen instantly in Home Assistant.

[![Open **Add device** in your Home Assistant instance.](https://my.home-assistant.io/badges/add_matter_device.svg)](https://my.home-assistant.io/redirect/add_matter_device/?domain=heiman)

[Learn more about Matter in Home Assistant.](/home-assistant/integrations/matter/)
