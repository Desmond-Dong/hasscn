[Frient](https://www.frient.com/) is a member of the Works with Home Assistant partner program for their Zigbee products. Frient is committed to making sure their products are up-to-date and ready to use in Home Assistant.

Frient Zigbee devices work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Zigbee integration in Home Assistant. As all connectivity happens locally, status updates and device control happen instantly in Home Assistant.

[![Open **Add device** in your Home Assistant instance.](https://my.home-assistant.io/badges/add_zigbee_device.svg)](https://my.home-assistant.io/redirect/add_zigbee_device/?brand=frient)

[Learn more about Zigbee in Home Assistant.](/home-assistant/integrations/zha/)
