## OpenTTS 2.0
------

Maybe the name still sounds fairly unknown to you, but [OpenTTS](https://github.com/synesthesiam/hassio-addons)
is an add-on, which gives you the possibility to use multiple 开源
text-to-speech systems. So that you can eventually have text spoken on: for
example, a Google Home speaker. [synesthesiam](https://github.com/synesthesiam)
recently released a new 版本 of OpenTTS and you can 安装 it as an
add-on in Home Assistant.

<a href="https://github.com/synesthesiam/hassio-addons"><img style="border: 0;" src="https://gh-卡片.dev/repos/synesthesiam/hassio-addons.svg"></a>

Give it a try!
