Today we are happy to announce our first Works with Home Assistant partner: Leviton!

[Leviton](https://leviton.com) is an American lighting, wiring devices, and load control company. Their products can be found in almost every home across the United 状态. As a partner, they will work with Nabu Casa to make sure that their line of Z-Wave 设备 will work great with Home Assistant. This gives users the best experience and will continue to give a great experience with automatic firmware updates via Home Assistant.

Leviton Z-Wave 设备 work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Z-Wave 集成 in Home Assistant (Z-Wave stick required). As all connectivity is happening locally, status updates and controlling your 设备 happen instantly in Home Assistant. Each 设备 that is connected to power will also act as a Z-Wave router to extend your Z-Wave mesh network.

<img src='/home-assistant/images/blog/2022-07-27-leviton-partner/leviton-devices.jpg' alt="Leviton Z-Wave Products" class='no-shadow'>
<br><br>

Leviton Decora Smart Z-Wave 设备 include their DZ6HD dimmer, DZ15S 开关, ZW4SF 风扇 Speed Controller, DZPD3 Plug-in Dimmer, DZPA1 Plug-in 开关, and ZW15R Outlet. All of these 设备 can be found on their [Amazon store](https://www.amazon.com/Leviton). These devices can then be used inside of your home to 打开 the 灯光 when you walk into a room, turn your bedside lamps on at a certain time, and much much more.
