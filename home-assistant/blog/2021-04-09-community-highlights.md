## Google Home

Long ago it was possible to read the 报警 and timers from your Google Home
in Home Assistant, due to a change in the API this was unfortunately no longer possible.

<object type="image/svg+xml" data="https://gh-卡片.dev/repos/leikoilja/ha-google-home.svg?link_target=_blank"></object>

But thanks to a new [custom 集成](https://github.com/leikoilja/ha-google-home)
from [leikoilja](https://github.com/leikoilja), it’s now possible to integrate 报警
or timers that you have set up on a Google Home 设备. For example, you could 开关
the 灯光 automatically in the morning based on an 报警 clock that you have set.
