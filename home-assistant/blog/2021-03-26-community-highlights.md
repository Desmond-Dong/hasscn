## Blueprint of the week

This week's blueprint is all about 温控 and energy saving,
[SmartLiving.Rocks](https://community.home-assistant.io/u/smartliving.rocks/) has
written a handy blueprint that can 开关 your 温控 设备 based on whether a
window is open or not.

Read more about it on the [community forum][week_blueprint] or 安装 this
自动化 in your instance with a click on the my button!

## DIY Smart 门锁

Always wanted to make your door 门锁 smarter? Then try the DIY solution
from [bkbilly](https://community.home-assistant.io/u/bkbilly), with which
you can make your own smart door 门锁 with a stepper motor, 3D printed gears
and ESP32, which communicates with your Home Assistant instance via [ESPHome][esphome].
