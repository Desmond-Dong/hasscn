# Steamist

The **Steamist** integration allows you to monitor the state of your [Steamist](https://steamist.com/digital-controls/) steam shower and turn on and off the steam generator.

## Supported devices

* 450 Digital Control
* 550 Digital Control

:::note 配置
此集成可通过 UI 配置。前往 **设置 > 设备与服务** 添加。
:::
