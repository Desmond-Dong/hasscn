# SwitchBot Matter

[SwitchBot Matter](https://www.switch-bot.com/) is a member of the Works with Home Assistant partner program for their Matter products. SwitchBot Matter is committed to making sure their products are up-to-date and ready to use in Home Assistant.

SwitchBot Matter Matter devices work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Matter integration in Home Assistant. As all connectivity happens locally, status updates and device control happen instantly in Home Assistant.

[![Open Add device in your Home Assistant instance.](https://my.home-assistant.io/badges/add_matter_device.svg)](https://my.home-assistant.io/redirect/add_matter_device/?domain=switchbot)

[Learn more about Matter in Home Assistant.](/home-assistant/integrations/matter/index.md)
