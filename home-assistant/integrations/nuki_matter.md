# Nuki Matter

[Nuki](https://nuki.io) is a member of the Works with Home Assistant partner program for their Matter products. Nuki is committed to making sure their products are up-to-date and ready to use in Home Assistant.

Nuki Matter devices work locally and integrate seamlessly with the Matter integration in Home Assistant. As all connectivity happens locally, status updates and device control happen instantly in Home Assistant.

[![Open Add device in your Home Assistant instance.](https://my.home-assistant.io/badges/add_matter_device.svg)](https://my.home-assistant.io/redirect/add_matter_device/?domain=nuki_matter)

[Learn more about Matter in Home Assistant.](/home-assistant/integrations/matter/index.md)

## 通过 Matter 集成设置 Nuki 需要 Thread

要使用此集成，您需要一个支持 Matter 的 Thread 边界路由器。有关更多信息，请参阅[Thread 文档](/home-assistant/integrations/thread/index.md)。您可以将您的 Home Assistant 安装变成 Thread 边界路由器，例如使用家庭助理 [Connect ZBT-1](https://www.home-assistant.io/connectzbt1/) 或 [ZBT-2](https://www.home-assistant.io/connect/zbt-2/)。有关设置的更多信息，请转到[将 Home Assistant 变成 Thread 边界路由器部分](/home-assistant/integrations/thread/index.md#turning-home-assistant-into-a-thread-border-router)

有关使用 Nuki 设备设置 Matter 的更多信息，请参阅 [Nuki Matter 设置指南](https://help.nuki.io/hc/en-001/articles/14596875392017-Setting-up-your-Matter-integration)。
